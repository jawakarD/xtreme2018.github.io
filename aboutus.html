Department of Mechatronics

The department of Mechatronics Engineering of KSRCT was started in the year 2002. The department was formed with the basic objective of providing skill based education in the field of mechanical and electronics. Right from the inception, the department has been offering well-built infrastructure facilities. The department offers a competent academic environment with a team of well-qualified faculty that ensures the students to excel in their technical exposure. The students are preparing to meet the requirements in the field of Mechatronics.

Objective

To train the students as competent professionals to keep pace with changes in automation needs in industry and research organization.
